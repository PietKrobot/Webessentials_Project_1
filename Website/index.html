IoT4Climate

We are IoT4Climate a group of students who are driven to find solution for climate problems. The world keeps evolving and so are we, modern problems call for modern solutions. We as IoT4Climate want to incorperate the complex problem of climate change and IoT.


What is our mission

We want to give researchers a better understanding of what the effects have been over the years. For researchers it isn't always easy to collect, transfer and compile/process data. This is where we step in as IoT4Climate to give our partners the technical resources necessary to give a better prediction of what the long therm consequences and solutions will be.

What is climate change

Is it difficult to understand what climate change is? No it is not difficult to understand what climate change is, climate change is a long-term change in the average weather patterns that have come to define Earth's local, regional and global climates.


The more difficult part of climate change is to know the effects climate change has on the planet and finding solution to solve te problems we face because of climate change. That is the difficult part of climate change, knowing why the world is changing and what solution we have to solve this complex issue.


What is our solution

Sensors

The start of every research begins with the gaterhing of data to get a view of what the situation is today to make a good prediction for tomorrow. At the moment we have a total of 4.000 sensors working and gathering data to see the effects of climate change all over the world.

Networking

Our networking solution will give us the needed infrastructure to collect a lot of different data from all over the world. A good network is essential to make our soltion work and to change the world for the better.

Store & proces data

All the data we gather will be stored on the servers. To make it possible for everyone who is working on the data to access it. Scientist all over the world will be able to use the data to make a prediction of what the future may bring to then be able to come up with solution for the climate change problems.


We as a small company won't be able to change te entire world. But every step taken in the right direction is one step forward, one step closer to a better future for everyone now and everyone to come.
